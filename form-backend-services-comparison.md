## Developer Perspective

| Feature | FormCare | Formspree | Basin | Self-hosted |
|----------|----------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Backend required | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Server maintenance | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Setup time | ~1 minute | ~2 minutes | ~2 minutes | Hours |
| File uploads | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | Depends |
| Dashboard | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | Depends |
| Cost for low-volume forms | Very low | Moderate | Moderate | Low |
| Cost for many client websites | Scales per usage | Can become expensive | Can become expensive | Server cost only |

## Summary

Form backend services allow developers to collect form submissions without building and maintaining a custom backend. Key differentiators are pricing, free-tier limits, integrations, and ease of setup.
